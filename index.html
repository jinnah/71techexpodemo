Phishing: Behind the Scene

What is Phishing?

Phishing is a type of cyber attack where attackers disguise themselves as a trustworthy entity to trick users into revealing sensitive information like passwords, bank details, or personal data.

How Does It Work?

Cybercriminals often use fake emails, malicious websites, and deceptive messages to lure victims into clicking harmful links or downloading malicious software.
